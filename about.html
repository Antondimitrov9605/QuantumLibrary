About Quantum Library

Quantum Library is the fastest search engine for scientific publications and open-access research materials. Our mission is to provide unrestricted access to knowledge for everyone.

Database Overview

Quantum Library currently provides access to over 306,571,849 scientific documents, freely available for search and retrieval.

Approximately 80% of the database consists of research articles published in journals, 6% are conference papers, and 5% are book chapters. The remaining content includes diverse academic materials.

Most research articles originate from medical and health sciences, followed by chemistry, biology, humanities, physics, and other fields.

Growth & Infrastructure

The database has expanded significantly over the years, with the largest growth occurring between 2013 and 2016. We continue to integrate new academic resources daily.

Due to the increasing number of searches, our servers experience high loads. To sustain and expand our infrastructure, we rely on community support.

Supporting Open Science

We integrate with multiple open-access databases, including:

  • arXiv - A repository for preprints in physics, mathematics, and computer science.
  • DOAJ - Directory of Open Access Journals covering all scientific disciplines.
  • CORE - Aggregator of millions of open-access research papers.
  • PubMed Central - Biomedical and life sciences publications.
  • Crossref Metadata - Indexing over 120 million academic papers.
